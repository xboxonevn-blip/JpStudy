# D2.Q2.3 Experiment - Minna N3+ Coverage Gap

## Experiment E2.3

Question: Is the fact that local Minna content stops at N4 a blocker for N3+ Vietnamese learners?

Method:

- Inspect local vocab route counts by level/series.
- Use official publisher sources to verify whether Minna itself has intermediate/Vietnamese materials.
- Use Vietnamese market/course pages as weak external evidence for how N3+ learners are routed.
- Attempt a Google Trends check; treat inability to retrieve a term-level VN comparison as a negative result, not evidence.

Local command:

```text
Get-ChildItem assets\data\content\vocab -Recurse -Filter *.json |
  group by level/series, excluding index.json
```

External sources:

- 3A FAQ: https://www.3anet.co.jp/en/faq.html
