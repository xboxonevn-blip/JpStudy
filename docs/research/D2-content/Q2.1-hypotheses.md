# D2.Q2.1 Hypotheses - Vietnamese Content Review Status

## H2.1.1 - Upper JLPT content carries concentrated Vietnamese review debt

Prediction: N1/N2 imported content has explicit machine-translation or human-review tags, while N3-N5 mostly lacks such status metadata. Effect size: at least 20% of N1/N2 items show `machine-translated-vi` or open review tags.

Falsifier: machine/open-review tags are evenly distributed across all levels or absent from N1/N2.

## H2.1.2 - Review debt is concentrated by content type

Prediction: grammar examples carry explicit open-review tags; grammar explanations carry approval signals; vocabulary carries machine-origin tags without equivalent approval/open-review status. Effect size: one dataset accounts for at least 80% of explicit open-review items.

Falsifier: open-review items are distributed across vocab, kanji, grammar, grammar examples, and immersion.

## H2.1.3 - March 2026 audits are stale for current scope

Prediction: current content scope exceeds the March 16 Vietnamese audit scope enough that old flagged-file counts cannot be used as current readiness evidence. Effect size: current JSON files scanned differ from old audit files scanned by more than 20%.

Falsifier: current scanner and March audit scan roughly the same number of files and datasets.
