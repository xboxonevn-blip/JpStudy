# D4.Q4.P5 Analysis - Sora N1

Commit: `e468d6c7`
Date: 2026-05-14

## Result

P5 is not production-ready for an N1-certified advanced reader. The app has real N1 immersion content after root initialization, but direct advanced routes fall to N5, study hub does not surface N1/news resources clearly, and news/current-world reading is absent.

## Hypothesis Updates

H4.P5.1 - N1 trust: falsified on live direct routes. Root shows N1; initialized hash navigation to immersion/study hub can preserve N1. Hard-loaded immersion, reading, study hub, vocab, and grammar fall to N5.

H4.P5.2 - Advanced reading: partially supported. N1 immersion has 25 decks and a readable advanced passage (`便利さの裏側にある依存`) with word annotations. JLPT reading hard load remains N5, and study hub does not highlight the N1 immersion path enough.

H4.P5.3 - News/current material: falsified. No news/current-world route or resource surfaced. N1 content appears to be `JpStudy Original` static passages.

H4.P5.4 - Review for certified user: partially supported. Root/progress/active surfaces provide maintenance framing (`Đọc một bài đắm mình`, `Chạy một block tổng hợp ngắn`, `Nhồi tối`). However, direct vocab/grammar still show beginner/foundations friction.

H4.P5.5 - Desktop throughput: partially supported. Layout is readable and sidebar routes are fast, but direct route state and weak advanced-resource surfacing break the advanced workflow.

## Severity

- HIGH: Live direct N1 routes fall to N5.
- HIGH: News/current-world reading is absent despite P5 need.
- HIGH: Study hub advanced filter does not expose N1 reading; it shows N3 grammar and listening only.
- MEDIUM: N1 immersion exists but is hidden behind root-initialized state or indirect navigation.
- MEDIUM: Certified-user review routes use generic empty-state/prototype language, not advanced maintenance confidence.

## What Worked

- Root N1/VI state works.
- N1 immersion content exists: 25 N1 decks and an advanced reader with furigana/annotation chips.
- The active workspace offers maintenance recipes and queue preview.
- Progress page offers a gentle review block instead of forcing a full exam flow.
- Desktop layout remains readable.

## Red Team

P5 intentionally used `reading` goal and Vietnamese UI. A Tokyo N1 user might prefer Japanese UI; that was not tested. Also, the N1 immersion route was reached by hash navigation after root init, not a normal visible button, because the accessible tree is sparse and the direct route is known stale. This proves N1 content exists, but not that real users can reliably discover it.

## Action

1. Redeploy/channel verify, then rerun direct route matrix for P2-P5.
2. Promote N1 immersion into study hub advanced resources and root reading CTAs.
3. Decide whether news/current-world reading is beta scope. If yes, add a clear news/current label and source policy; if no, remove that persona promise.
4. Add an advanced-reader smoke path: root N1 -> immersion N1 -> open deck -> reader annotations.
5. After P5, synthesize D4 cross-persona findings before local feature work.

## Mental Model

Sora không cần học lại N5; cô cần nội dung đủ khó và đường vào ổn định. Khi app đã nạp đúng N1, immersion chứng minh nội dung nâng cao có thật. Nhưng nếu link thẳng và study hub vẫn nói N5/N3, người học N1 sẽ không đào sâu đủ lâu để tìm phần tốt đó.
