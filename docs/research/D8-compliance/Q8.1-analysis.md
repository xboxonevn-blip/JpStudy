# D8.Q8.1 Analysis - Privacy And Terms Coverage

## E8.2 Result

D8.Q8.1 is not launch-ready. The app has Firebase Auth, Analytics, cloud-backup/data controls, and account sign-in surfaces, but no discovered `/privacy` or `/terms` route constants, no GoRouter routes, no Vietnamese/English policy copy, and no policy links from Settings, Onboarding, or Login.

## E8.3 Implementation Update

The route/link surface is no longer absent. A minimal beta legal slice now exists:

- `/privacy` and `/terms` route constants and GoRouter entries.
- Shared `LegalDocumentScreen` with Vietnamese and English Privacy Policy / Terms draft copy.
- Visible links from Login, Onboarding, Me settings, and Data controls.
- Focused route and widget tests for the new links and routes.

This clears the product-surface absence found in E8.2, but it does not clear legal sufficiency. All copy remains marked `review-needed draft`; public launch still needs final human/legal review, support/contact details, and deletion-policy confirmation.

## E8.2 Findings

1. `H8.1.1` falsified. `AppRoutePath` has no `/privacy` or `/terms`; `meta_routes.dart` has no legal routes.
2. `H8.1.2` falsified. `app_language.dart` has "terms" only in vocabulary count copy; no discovered Privacy Policy or Terms of Service copy in VI/EN.
3. `H8.1.3` falsified. `MeScreen` and `DataSettingsScreen` expose account/data controls but no Privacy or Terms links.
4. `H8.1.4` falsified. `OnboardingScreen` has level, goal, and first-win pages only; no legal links before completion.
5. `H8.1.5` falsified. `LoginDialog` supports Google/email sign-in but no policy links near the sign-in actions.

## E8.3 Findings

1. `H8.1.1` now has implementation evidence: `AppRoutePath.privacy`, `AppRoutePath.terms`, `AppRouteName.privacy`, `AppRouteName.terms`, and matching GoRoutes exist.
2. `H8.1.2` now has draft evidence: `AppLanguage` exposes Privacy/Terms titles, draft notices, and VI/EN body text.
3. `H8.1.3` now has link evidence: `MeScreen` and `DataSettingsScreen` render `LegalDocumentLinks`.
4. `H8.1.4` now has link evidence: first onboarding page renders compact legal links.
5. `H8.1.5` now has link evidence: `LoginDialog` renders compact legal links and remains scrollable in small test viewports.

## Beta Impact

Do not treat the app as public launch-compliance ready. The route/link blocker is reduced to a legal-review blocker: copy is intentionally marked review-needed, and operational details such as support contact and data-deletion process need confirmation.

## Red Team

Steelman opposite: a tiny private friend beta might operate with external docs or manual disclosure outside the app. That does not satisfy the stated requirement because the routes and in-app links are absent. It also fails the "6-month rerun" standard: external/manual disclosure is not reproducible from the repository.

## Decision

Next implementation should be a small legal-surface slice, not a broad compliance rewrite:

1. Add route constants and GoRouter entries for `/privacy` and `/terms`.
2. Add a shared `LegalDocumentScreen` or two small screens with VI + EN text.
3. Link both documents from `MeScreen`/`DataSettingsScreen`, `OnboardingScreen`, and `LoginDialog`.
4. Add route smoke/widget tests that assert the routes and links exist.
5. Keep legal text draft marked review-needed until human/legal review.

E8.3 implemented this minimal slice. Next decision: keep it as beta draft only until final legal copy and support/deletion details are approved.
