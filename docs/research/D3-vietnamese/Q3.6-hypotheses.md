# D3.Q3.6 Hypotheses - Plural and ICU Risk

Commit: `8387e444`

## Question

Vietnamese generally does not require plural inflection, but English does. Where might pluralization be silently wrong for English users under the current `AppLanguage` model?

## Hypotheses

H3.6.1: The app does not use `Intl.plural` or generated ICU messages today.

H3.6.2: English plural risk is common enough to matter: at least `30` English count/interpolation strings use raw plural nouns such as `items`, `terms`, `questions`, `lessons`, or `strokes`.

H3.6.3: Only a small minority of English count strings manually guard singular/plural with `count == 1`.

H3.6.4: The highest-risk user-visible defects are simple `1 items` / `1 terms` / `1 questions` labels, not complex Vietnamese plural issues.
